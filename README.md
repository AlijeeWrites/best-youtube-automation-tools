## How to Stay Safe with YouTube’s 2026 AI Policy

YouTube explicitly supports AI‑assisted content, but **transparency is required.**

### 1. The Altered Content Label
If your AI video looks like a real person or place, you must mark it during upload.
* **The Rule:** Disclose content that makes a real person appear to do something they didn't, or generates a realistic scene that didn't happen.
* **The Result:** A label appears in your description. This **does not** limit your reach or monetization.

### 2. Avoid 1‑Click Spam
YouTube’s 2026 algorithm includes a “low‑effort” filter. 
* **Pro Tip:** Use InVideo’s **Magic Box** to tweak scenes or add a custom intro. This proves human creative "steering," keeping your channel safe.

### 3. Use Your Own Digital Voice
To stand out and reduce repetitive content flags, use the **voice cloning** feature. Creating a unique audio identity helps with viewer engagement and algorithm trust.
